01. Service:

Photography

Our photography services are structured as an ongoing monthly subscription model, where all clients have access to "unlimited" photography hours. You can purchase 2-hour, 4-hour, 8-hour, and 16-hour packages, where the package size represents the maximum amount of hours you'll be able to reserve on the photographer's calendar, at any one time. What makes the service "unlimited", is that once you use hours, or cancel a reservation, those hours immediately become available again. Our goal here is to offer restaurants easy access to high-quality content, where the focus is teamwork, not billable hours. If you're interested, or have questions, please feel free to contact us!
