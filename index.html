Leverage the power of influence

We run hands-on influencer marketing campaigns for businesses launching a product, promoting an event, or simply wanting to boost brand exposure.

Why influencer marketing?

Opt-in Audiences

People follow influencers because they've chosen to be part of a community that interests them. Influencers are trusted, have earned authority, and provide a more native experience over invasive ads.

Content Creators

Influencer marketing isn’t just about paying for exposure, it’s about gaining exposure and creating premium content. This content sticks around long after influencers create it, and can be reused on your own social feeds.

Amazing Value

All things considered, influencer marketing can have some of the lowest costs, highest engagement, and most effective returns of any digital (and traditional) marketing channel!

How does it work?

1. Game Plan

How can we best position your brand for success? It’s time to think strategy. We define the objectives for your campaign, put together a creative pitch for influencers, and ensure you have all the infrastructure in place to hit campaign goals.

2. Discovery

Finding the best influencers is crutial! We conduct a lot of influencer discovery, while also leveraging our existing database. Our goal is to find and match your brand with the influencers that'll champion your campaign’s objectives.

3. Manage & Report

Here's the really fun part - we take care of hiring, contracting, and paying influencers. We even ensure influencers comply with FTC endorsment requirements. After the campaign goes live, we monitor influencer's posts, and report all key metrics to you.

What's the cost?

Our pricing model is performance-based - this way you only pay for real results, not how many hours we work. Our services are also suitable for smaller campaigns, and can easily scale as your ambitions grow. If you’re looking for exact numbers, feel free to reach out to us!
