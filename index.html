Social Media Management

for Food & Beverage Businesses

We manage photography, creative content, driving engagement, growing followers, running paid ads, and organizing hands-on influencer marketing campaigns - everything to keep your brand relevant!

What makes us different?

We really know social! We're a boutique agency that started with a foodie instagram - @MunchATX. This beginning gave us a valuable perspective and a hands-on understanding of how the food & beverage industry operates on social. As a bonus, it also allows us to leverage our local influence to help promote our clients! 😉

01. Service:

Photography

We take beautiful professional photos that showcase your product, generate awareness, appeal to foodies, and ultimately generate the sales you're looking for. Food & drink is our specialty, so it's our mission to get people visually excited about what you're cookin' up!

02. Service:

Creative Content

We create awesome content that goes beyond photography, including: custom graphics, impactful copy, leveraging effective hashtags, and delivering the right information where it counts. We help your brand find and establish its unique voice!

03. Service:

Engagement

We always make sure we're a part of the conversation - if that's promptly answering DMs, sharing customer photos, participating in the comments, or even thanking customers for coming in. Doing this brings a personal touch to your business, strengthens your community, and helps personify your brand.

04. Service:

Build Community

Followers aren't just a number, they're the people & businesses that your voice brings value to. They compose the community around your product, and having a large & genuine audience to champion your brand is important - let us help you maximize that value and grow that audience!

05. Service:

Paid Media

We run paid ads to boost brand awareness, promote a new product launch, or create extra buzz for an event. Our process includes crafting actionable messaging, testing multiple approaches, targeting new customer segments, and actively monitoring campaign data to adjust spending in the right places.

06. Service:

Influencer Marketing

We run hands-on influencer marketing efforts - coordinating with high-quality influencers that will champion your goals. We manage all discovery, outreach, scheduling, compensation, and even appropriate FTC endorsement requirements.
